# REGULON - Demo Video Script & One-Liner Statements

## Business Model Overview

**REGULON** is an AI-powered, human-verified regulatory execution platform that automates compliance across Indian regulators (MCA, GST, Income Tax, RBI, SEBI) with mandatory professional verification by licensed CAs and lawyers before submission.
