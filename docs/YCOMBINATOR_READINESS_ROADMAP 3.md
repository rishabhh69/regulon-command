## 🎯 Critical Success Factors

1. **Traction is Everything**: Get to 20+ paying customers before applying. Y Combinator wants to see growth.

2. **Founder Credibility**: Tell your personal story. Why are YOU the right person to build this? Your background matters.

3. **Market Size**: Be specific. "Indian CA compliance software market is ₹3,000Cr". Not vague.

4. **Unit Economics**: Show CAC, LTV, payback period. If LTV:CAC is >3:1, you have a viable business.

5. **Unfair Advantage**: Why can only you win? "Regulatory expertise" is better than "we built it first".

6. **Clear Use of Funds**: "₹2Cr to hire 5 engineers, acquire 1,000 customers, reach ₹1Cr MRR". Be specific.

7. **Demo Works**: Product must work smoothly. If demo crashes, you lose.

8. **Y Combinator Fit**: Emphasize: Global ambitions, large market, scalable software, unique founding story.
