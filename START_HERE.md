## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Should I build all 49 items or just Phase 1?
**A**: Phase 1 only = 70% complete, not market-ready. You need Phase 1-4 minimum for launch. Phase 5 is optional for v1.0.

### Q: Can I parallelize development?
**A**: Yes! Phase 2 and 3 can happen in parallel (different teams). See IMPLEMENTATION_QUICK_START.md for team allocation.

### Q: What if I only have 2 developers?
**A**: Timeline stretches from 15 weeks to 24 weeks. Each developer handles 24-25 prompts sequentially. Quality may suffer → recommend waiting to hire more devs.

### Q: How long to complete one prompt?
**A**: Typically 3-5 days: Day 1 understand, Day 2-3 implement, Day 4 test, Day 5 review/merge.

### Q: Can Copilot really generate this much code?
**A**: Yes. The prompts are designed based on Copilot's capabilities. Each prompt generates 200-500 lines of production-ready code. Developers review/integrate.

### Q: What if government portal APIs aren't available?
**A**: Start Phase 3 with mock data. When APIs available, swap in real implementations. Critical path not blocked.

### Q: Is this for enterprise or startup?
**A**: Both. Architecture scales from 10 users (startup) to 1000 users (enterprise). AWS scaling handles it.

### Q: What about multi-language support?
**A**: Not in this phase (assume English-only for v1.0). Hindi translation can be Phase 5 enhancement.
